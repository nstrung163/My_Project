Group Rates

These discounted rates are available for schools, churches, scouts, day cares, athletic organizations, and non-profit organizations. In order to receive these rates, the trip must be set up in advance. A minimum of 15 group participants required, and a maximum of 500 for these rates. For groups that would like the park to themselves, wish to visit on a date we are not already scheduled to open, or for larger groups of up to 2000 participants, please click here to see our private events page.

Please call +70 2 402 434 to schedule your next field trip or outing!

Extreme Group Trip

Minimum 15 Participating Group Members

$15/Person for 4 hours

$18/Person for 6 hours

or $20/person for All Day

Unlimited Access to All Attractions: Switchback Roller Coaster, Dizzy Toucan, Bungee Trampoline, Parachute Drop, Rock-Climbing Wall, Silo Climb, MaxFlight Simulator, Jungle Playland, Video Games, Go-Karts, Mad Raft*, Viper*, and Viper's Tail*.

Add Pizza for $5 per person (2 slices of pizza and a medium drink each).

One adult wristband will be provided free for every 10 children/group members to participate. Other adults (18+) may come in for free to watch. Additional adult bands are the same price as the children's bands for those wishing to participate.

For further information or to book a group, please call +70 2 402 434.

For weekdays in May only: Because of the limited number of schools that can be booked on each field trip day, a deposit of $100 will be required to reserve your spot on any of the May field trip dates.

*Water rides available March-September, weather permitting.
