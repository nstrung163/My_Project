Recommendation: Those who are weak heart, afraid of heights and speed should not play this game.
In addition, to ensure safety, Phi Long Than Toc has a height limit of participants with a minimum height of 140 cm.

Some suggestions when playing at Merciado Amusement Park

Merciado Amusement Park is open from 14h-19h from Monday to Friday, on Saturday & Sunday from 9-19h. Depending on the travel time, you should have reasonable arrangements. Some tips when going out to Merciado Amusement Park:

  • Should go in the morning if going on the weekend: Because the weekend visitors to Merciado Amusement Park are quite crowded, to avoid the situation of queuing for too long, you should go in the morning. Playing in the morning is less harsh than the afternoon.
  • Do not eat before playing thrilling games
  • Bring snacks to play Merciado Amusement Park
  • Play low to high rank games and relax with low intensity games
  • Use sunscreen carefully to avoid skin diseases
