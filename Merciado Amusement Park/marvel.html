Find your power at Marvel Season of Super Heroes.

Join Marvel’s greatest for some mightily epic experiences at Meciado Amusement Park, from 16th March to 10th June 2020.

Do you have the courage of Captain America, the agility of Spider-Man or the fearlessness of Black Widow?

Feel the power of the epic Marvel: Super Heroes United show Immerse in the origins of greatness with this epic indoor show super-charged with live Super Heroes and ground-smashing, heroic special effects. Feel your Spider-Sense tingle as Marvel legends Iron Man and Captain America battle each other before uniting with Spider-Man, Black Widow and Hulk to bring down the baddest villain in the Marvel universe, Thanos.
See a show of the future in Stark Expo: Make Way for a Better Tomorrow! Swoop into the middle of the mayhem with this electrifying extravaganza buzzing with Marvel Super Heroes. Gaze in awe as The Avengers, including Thor and Black Widow, fight a colossal clash with Asgard’s evil second prince, Loki. And brace yourself for the wonder of Captain Marvel dropping in to save the day!
Stand proud at Heroic Encounter: Captain America Stand tall and perfect your salute with America’s first Avenger. He’s on the hunt for new recruits and he can’t wait for a heroic handshake with an honourable fan, like you.
NEW! Unleash your inner guardian at Heroic Encounter: Star-Lord and Gamora Drop into Production Courtyard and power up for a mighty meeting with one of the Guardians of the Galaxy. If Gamora is waiting for you, then make sure you have your combat skills locked and loaded. If it’s Star-Lord, you better have your grooviest dance moves at the ready.
Hail the magnificence of four majestic Marvel Colossal Statues Feel the awesome aura of an eye-popping Marvel atmosphere in Meciado Amusenment Park. And take in the tremendous sight of the colossal statues of four worthy Super Heroes: Hulk, Black Panther, Iron Man and Captain Marvel.
