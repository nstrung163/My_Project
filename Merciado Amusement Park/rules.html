Season Pass Rules

This pass is non-transferable, non-refundable, and not for resale. Use by anyone other than the pass holder whose photo or finger scan does not match the records associated with the pass will result in expulsion and/or revocation of the pass without refund. Merciado Amusement Park reserves the right to revoke this pass without refund for any reason it deems necessary. Upon entry to the park on each regular operating day, this pass provides the pass holder with an “unlimited band”, which includes all rides, video games, and attractions for the day. This pass cannot be used towards birthday party rates, group rates, or any other discounted rates.

Note: All rules are subject to change at the discretion of park management.

Premium Pass Rules

General Pass Rules: This pass is non-transferable, non-refundable, and not for resale. Use by anyone other than the pass holder whose photo or finger scan does not match the records associated with the pass will result in expulsion and/or revocation of the pass without refund. Merciado Amusement Park reserves the right to revoke this pass without refund for any reason it deems necessary. Upon entry to the park on each regular operating day, this pass provides the pass holder with an “unlimited band”, which includes all rides, video games, and attractions for the day. This pass cannot be used towards birthday party rates, group rates, or any other discounted rates.

CLAIMING YOUR 20% DISCOUNT FOR FRIENDS: Pass holder must be present and provide a valid finger scan to claim the 20% discount on up to 6 unlimited or indoor wristbands per day for other guests. This discount is not valid for online purchases, cannot be combined with other discounts, and cannot be used towards other passes. Solicitation to other guests not known by the pass holder, or attempting to profit off this discount in any other way through other guests will result in revocation of the pass without a refund.

CLAIMING YOUR 20% DISCOUNT ON FOOD AND MERCHANDISE: Upon entry, the pass holder will receive a “premium band” which will entitle that person to a 20% discount on all food and merchandise purchased in the park. The person wearing this band must be present at the time of each purchase to claim this discount, and must provide a valid finger scan at the time of purchase if it is requested. Solicitation to other guests not known by the pass holder, or attempting to profit off this discount in any way, will result in revocation of the pass without refund. Attempting to transfer the band to another person will also result in revocation of the pass without a refund.

BRING A FRIEND DAYS: On each bring a friend day, each premium pass holder or member will be allowed to bring in one friend at the discounted rate. Some bring a friend days are free, while others are discounted. Merciado Amusement Park will have at least 1 Bring a Friend Free Day each month in addition to the Bring a Friend for a Discount days, the dates of which will be posted on our website. Merciado Amusement Park reserves the right to change the dates of these days. Full Bring a Friend Calendar: merciadoamusementpark.com/premium-calendar

Note: All rules are subject to change at the discretion of park management.
